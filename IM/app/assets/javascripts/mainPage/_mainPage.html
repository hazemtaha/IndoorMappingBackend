Our Goal

Our goal is show where everything is in large building complexes, such as universities, hospitals, conference venues and shopping malls. By solving this problem, we are able to help people find their way around.

How can we help?


If you are a building owner you can create an account , regist your own building and draw floors maps and set beacons on blocks , to help your building visitors to get their target faster.

For Owners

If you are a building visitor you can download our mobile application create an account , and start to search for your target block inside building.

For Visitors
